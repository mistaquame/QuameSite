Why QuameVoice?

🔒

100% Local & Private

Runs entirely offline using whisper.cpp. No cloud APIs, no audio ever leaves your machine.

⚡

Blazing Fast

whisper.cpp is 2–5× faster than Python Whisper with ~¼ the RAM. Transcription finishes before you lift your finger.

⌨️

Push-to-Talk Hotkey

Press Alt+Shift+Space to start recording, press again to stop and paste. Fully configurable to any Ctrl/Alt combination.

📋

Automatic Paste

Restores focus to your previous window and pastes the transcribed text — no context switching, no extra clicks.

🌍

21 Languages

Auto-detect or choose from 21 supported languages including English, Deutsch, Français, Español, 中文, 日本語, and more.

🪟

Cross-Platform

Windows fully supported with installer. Linux and macOS builds available — build from source or use the release packages.

How It Works

1

Press the hotkey

Press Alt+Shift+Space (or your custom hotkey). The overlay shows Recording…

2

Speak naturally

Speak into your microphone. Press Escape anytime to cancel.

3

Press again to transcribe

Press the hotkey again. Recording stops, transcription begins locally.

4

Text appears at your cursor

Focus is restored to your previous window and the text is pasted automatically.
